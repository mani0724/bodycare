What We Do

Hair Botox

The Hair Botox treatment works by filling in the individual fibre of hair to help give it fullness and make it smooth. Botox is injected into the scalp which relaxes the muscle, enhancing blood flow and increasing the delivery of nutrients to the hair.

PRP Hair Regrowth Treatment

PRP, also known as Platelet-Rich Plasma treatment for hair loss, is a medical treatment in which your blood is drawn, processed, and then injected into the scalp. Your cosmetologist will take a small amount of your blood and use a centrifuge to separate the plasma from it.

GFC Hair Loss Treatment

Regain lost hair and reduce hair fall! The GFC hair loss treatment can improve hair thickness, strengthen the hair shaft and stimulate hair growth. GFC uses the activated growth factors from your blood platelets to trigger the growth phase in the hormonally challenged hair roots.

QR678® Hair Growth Treatment

Get thicker, longer and stronger hair! QR678® is a revolutionary hair regrowth treatment that provides effective, permanent and natural-looking hair regrowth benefits. This innovative and advanced treatment can prevent and reverse hair loss, as well as stop hair fall and boost hair regrowth.

Fillers

Dermal Fillers are gel-like substances injected beneath the skin to help restore youthful skin, smooth facial creases, restore lost volume and enhance contours.

Laser Hair Reduction

Experience freedom from excessive hair growth with our Laser Hair Reduction service. The Laser Hair Reduction treatment at Bodycraft Clinic helps you remove unwanted hair from different parts of the body. It is a painless, safe and permanent alternative to other messy and painful hair reduction methods.
